San Andreas Criminal Code

The justice system works as follows: If you are caught committing a crime or driving violation the sentence will be decided by the arresting officer based on the Criminal Code, situation and the offenders previous record. The accused has the option to fight the case by a jury of their peers providing they have somewhat of a case. If the case is lost the accused will be sentenced to double the time of the original sentence. This applies for criminal and driving offences. If you chose this route you will be placed on bail providing you have the correct amount of money for bail. While on bail you may be subject to conditions for your release (if your crime was driving related maybe you'll be banned from driving till your trial date, if it booze related maybe you can't drink, curfew, etc.) In order for fair trial there must be six people willing to sit and hear the case and evidence provided by the defendant and the officer on the case. The peers will make their judgement and a judge will make the finally call on the sentence. If the peers feel you're not guilty then your free to go. You are free to hire a lawyer at any point. If you accept the sentence at the time of arrest you may pay your fine on the spot or serve your community service providing you've been charged with an offence that doesn't require prison. If prison time is required you time is to be spent in the boiling broke pen and your sentence time start after your admitted. Any sentence issued in the court will be served in the prison as well. Jail break outs are permitted as long as there are minimum two guards on duty watching the jail/prison in question. No road break outs are permitted. By failing roleplay by any order made by police or judge and not following roleplay you will be subject to Midnight Role Play disciplinary process.

PENAL CODE (1)-Title 1: Major Crime (Chapter)-01 to (Chapter)-07)

Chapter 1: Murder

The unlawful killing of a human being with malice aforethought

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 35 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 40 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 50 months.
Killing of an LEO warrants an extension of 10 months to the offense.

Chapter 2: Attempted murder

Demonstration of an intent to murder, meaning that the perpetrator either tried to murder and failed or took a substantial step towards committing a murder

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 40 months.

Chapter 3: Aggravated battery

Intentionally touching or striking another person and, in doing so: Intentionally or knowingly caused great bodily harm, permanent disability, or permanent disfigurement to the victim.

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.

Chapter 4: Kidnapping

Intentional or forcible abduction or confinement of an individual without their consent to: hold for ransom, use in the commission of another criminal act, use as hostage, assault, using a firearm as a tool to threat or attempt to force abduction.

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.

Chapter 5: Assault

Intentionally, knowingly or recklessly causing harm or bodily injury to another; or causing contact with another person and should reasonably know that the other will regard such contact as offensive or provocative

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Assault of an LEO warrants and extension of 5 months to the offense.

Chapter 6: Aiding and abetting a suspect

Someone criminally responsible for the unlawful conduct of another person(s) or organization if aiding, promoting or is otherwise part of the criminal offense

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 7: Arson

Deliberate destruction of property by means of fire, incendiary or explosive device.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 1: Fabrication of an illegal substance

Illegally facilitating the production or creation of an illegal substance

Sentence:

Narcotics of any quality shall be ran through mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.

Falsely presenting oneself as or acting in the capacity of a public servant, government official or attorney.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.

Theft and/or causing bodily injury using a deadly weapon during the commission of a crime.

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.

Chapter 4: Robbery

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.

Possession with the intent to distribute of an illegal weapon (firearm, melee or explosive)

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.

Chapter 6: Possession with the intent to distribute of an illegal substance

Possession with the intent to distribute of an illegal substance of any kind

Sentence:

First offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.
Second offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 20 months.
Third offense: Mandatory imprisonment of 25 months.
Further offenses: Mandatory imprisonment of 30 months.

Threatening violence to a person(s), organization(s) or property with the intent to provoke a reaction by impact, interrupt EMS/Fire or law enforcement, prevent the use of a building, incite fear in the public, influence the conduct of polices of the government or its entities.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 15 months.

Chapter 9: Possession of an illegal substance

Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.

Fleeing or attempting to flee an LEO who is attempting to arrest or detain

Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.

Chapter 12: Negligent discharge of a firearm

Discharging a firearm without any valid reason in doing so

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 13: Possession of an illegal weapon

Having possession of an illegal weapon

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 14: Possession of a stolen vehicle

Having possession of a stolen vehicle

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 15: Hit and run

Failure to stop and remain at the scene of an accident after having caused it

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 16: Prostitution

Exchange of a sexual act for money

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 17: Stalking

Unwanted pursuit of another person

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 18: Fraud

Individual involved in acts intended to swindle people

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 19: brandishing a deadly weapon in city limits

Unlawful display or draw of a deadly weapon of any kind capable of producing bodily harm,

Exception:

PENAL CODE (3)-Title 3: Minor Crime (Chapter)-01 to (Chapter)-17)

Chapter 1: Sexual harassment

Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, and or other verbal or physical conduct of sexual nature constitute sexual harassment when submission to or rejection of this conduct explicitly or implicitly affects and individuals employment, unreasonably interferes with an individuals work performance or creates an intimidating hostile or offensive work environment.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 10 months.

Chapter 2: Resisting arrest, searches, detention

Resisting arrest search or detention by an LEO officer

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 8 months.

Chapter 3: Destruction of government property

One that pursues the destruction of government property such as buildings or vehicles.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 4: Obstruction of justice

Interference with the orderly administration of law and justice which includes the following: injuring an officer, obstruction of a criminal investigation, tampering with witnesses, victim or informant, retaliating against witnesses victims or informants.

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 5: Trespassing on government property

Entering of government property without consent from government officials

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 6: Trespassing on private property

Entering of government property without consent from landlord/ owner official

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 7: Bribery or Extortion

Influence or alter the actions of an individual

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 8: Conspiracy

Two or more individuals agree to commit an unlawful act taking action toward its completion

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 9: Disorderly conduct

Disturbing the peace of an area by obnoxious or unruly conduct

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 10: Driving under the influence

Operating of a motor vehicle while being intoxicated

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 11: Violating a restraining order

Failing to obey a restraining order

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 12: False imprisonment

Intentionally restraining another by physical force or by the threat of physical force without privilege or authority

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 13: Public intoxication

Being drunk or under the influence of drugs in public

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 14: Failure to pay citations

Failure to pay citation past the grace period of 3 days

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.
10 or more citations become a bench warrant

Chapter 15: False statement to law enforcement

Providing false statements or information to LEO with the knowledge of their falsehood

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months.

Chapter 16: Destruction of private property

Destruction of property that is not owned by the individual

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months.

Chapter 17: Destruction of public property

Destruction of property that is owned by the public

Sentence:

Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months.

PENAL CODE (4)-Title 4: Traffic Violations (Chapter)01 to (Chapter)-07)

Chapter 1: Street Racing

Racing a motor vehicle on a public road or a highway unless it’s a licensed event. Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 8 months.

Chapter 2: Driving with a suspended license

Being a driver with a suspended license Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 3: Felony speeding 30+

Speeding on a public road 30 MPH over the speed limit Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months.

Chapter 4: Reckless driving

Operating a motor vehicle with the intention to disregard the safety of others or their property Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 7 months. And can be obligated to pay for damages caused to others.

Chapter 5: Failure to obey traffic control device

Pedestrian or driver of a motor vehicle that disregarded a traffic control device Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months.

Chapter 6: Illegally off-roading

A driver operating a motor vehicle disregarded public property not meant for off-roading can be classified as disturbing the peace/disorderly conduct Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months. Discretion can be used.

Chapter 7: Driving without a license

A driver that is operating a motor vehicle without a valid license Sentence: Mandatory imprisonment of 3 months. Discretion can be used.
